Candlestick Patterns

Bullish Candlesticks

Bullish Engulfing

Bullish

A large green candle completely engulfs the previous small red candle, indicating a potential reversal to an uptrend.

Hammer

Bullish

A small body with a long lower wick, found after a downtrend, signaling a possible bullish reversal.

Tweezer Bottoms

Bullish

Two or more candlesticks with nearly identical lows, indicating support and potential reversal upward.

Bearish Candlesticks

Bearish Engulfing

Bearish

A large red candle completely engulfs the previous small green candle, indicating a potential reversal to a downtrend.

Shooting Star

Bearish

A small body with a long upper wick, found after an uptrend, indicating a possible bearish reversal.

Tweezer Tops

Bearish

Two or more candlesticks with nearly identical highs, indicating resistance and potential reversal downward.
