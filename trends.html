Trend Patterns

Bullish Trend Patterns

Ascending Triangle

Bullish

Horizontal resistance line with rising lows, showing buyers gaining strength and a likely upward breakout.

Symmetrical Triangle (Bullish)

Bullish

Converging trendlines with a breakout to the upside, suggesting a continuation of an uptrend.

Falling Wedge

Bullish

Downward sloping converging lines, price breaks upward, indicating a reversal to an uptrend.

Cup and Handle

Bullish

Rounded bottom followed by a short pullback, then a breakout upward, signaling continued bullishness.

Double Bottom

Bullish

Two lows at similar price levels form a "W" shape, indicating strong support and a reversal upward.

Bearish Trend Patterns

Descending Triangle

Bearish

Horizontal support line with falling highs, showing sellers in control and a likely downward breakout.

Symmetrical Triangle (Bearish)

Bearish

Converging trendlines with a breakout to the downside, suggesting a continuation of a downtrend.

Rising Wedge

Bearish

Upward sloping converging lines, price breaks downward, indicating a reversal to a downtrend.

Head and Shoulders

Bearish

Three peaks, the middle being the highest, signals a trend reversal from bullish to bearish.

Double Top

Bearish

Two highs at similar price levels form an "M" shape, indicating strong resistance and a reversal downward.
